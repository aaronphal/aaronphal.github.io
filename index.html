About Me

Hi, my name is Aaron Phalphouvong. I am an aspiring programmer and photographer. I am currently a Sophomore at Hamilton High School in Sussex, Wisconsin. I first got taste of programming when I was in the fifth grade. My oldest brother was vising one day and told me that I should learn coding. I did not know what he meant at first. I took it to consideration a few weeks later, I decided to go find tutorials on YouTube. I first learned the Java programming language from those videos. I then joined the FIRST LEGO LEAGUE team in the Sixth grade thinking I would learn real programming languages. After my first year on the team I never came back. After quiting robotics in Sixth grade, I learned the basics of Web Programming. In Seventh grade, I took a break from all programming. after about two years, I joined FRC 537 robotics team as a code member in high school. I re-learned Java from programming the robot. Then I had friends that gave me the idea of learning C++. I've Always been wanting to learn C++ but never knew where to start. From then on I became proficient in Java and am currently learning C++!
